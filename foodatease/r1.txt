INGREDIENTS:
3 avocados
2onions(finely chopped)
2 tomatoes(chopped)
Salt( as per your taste)
Lemon juice(2 tbsp)
Optional ingredients
Pumpkin seeds,watermelon seeds,muskmelon seeds {these ingredients will give a nice crunch)
PROCESS:
Mash the avocados , addall theother ingredients and mix!
AS SIMPLEAS THAT!
USES;
Guacamole and nachos are a classic
Guacamole can also be used as a sandwich spread[WARNING! DO NOT HEAT THE GUACAMOLE BECUSE IT
WILL BECOME BITTER]if you want a toasted sandwich, toast the bread and then put the guacamole.
