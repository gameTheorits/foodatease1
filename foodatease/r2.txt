INGREDIENTS
● ¾ cup All purpose flour
● ¼ cup Cornmeal
● ⅔ cup lukewarm water
● 1½ tsp salt
● 3-4 tbsp oil
PROCESS
❖ Add all the dry ingredients in a bowl and mix
❖ Then, add the water bit by bit and knead it into a dough
❖ Glaze the dough with the oil and let it rest for 10 mins
❖ Cut this dough into 8-10 equal parts and roll each piece into nice flat circles
❖ Then heat it on a griddle and serve hot
You can use this to make wraps,quesadillas,soft tacos,and much more
